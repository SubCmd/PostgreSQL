Q5. 아래 테이블과 SQL의 실행 결과는?

[SALES]
DEPT | AMT
-----+-----
A    | 100
A    | 200
B    | 300
B    | NULL
C    | 400

```sql
SELECT DEPT, SUM(AMT) AS TOTAL
FROM SALES
GROUP BY DEPT
HAVING COUNT(*) >= 2;
```

① A/300, B/300 ② A/300 만 출력 ③ A/300, B/300, C/400 ④ A/300, B/NULL
